= IDE's

This page allows you to open the IDE of your choice.

.IDE's
image::images/ides_page/ides.png["IDE's", width="600", link="images/ides_page/ides.png"]

It currently lists only 3 IDEs: Eclipse, IntelliJ and VS Code.

It will open the IDE from the devonfw instance that you have selected in the top bar:

.Choose your devonfw instance
image::images/ides_page/devonfw-instance-dropdown.png["Choose your devonfw instance", width="300", link="images/ides_page/devonfw-instance-dropdown.png"]

Click on *OPEN* to launch the IDE of your choice. By default, the IDE will display projects from your main workspace in the selected devonfw instance.

image::images/ides_page/3-ides.png["Open an IDE", width="600", link="images/ides_page/3-ides.png"]
